My name is Caitlin Shea and I am a writer, artist, and UX designer. I have a BFA in Dramatic Writing from Savannah College of Art and Design, and a Certificate in UI/UX Design from Georgia Institute of Technology. I am passionate about storytelling and designing as a way to make the world a brighter and more interesting place.
